What's with the superman logo?

This website actually takes its inspiration from a therapist known as Drew Davis. Known for his magnetic personality, he specializes in helping youth with depression, addiction, processing disorders, and more.



If you step into his office, it's decorated with everything superman. He even wears a superman shirt underneath his regular shirt every day. Drew has a long list of clients who are grateful to him for the aid he's given. He's truly responsible for saving those who needed saving.



We hope the therapists and patients who use this service can also feel similar feelings as they walk the road of self-improvement and healing.
